AuraExplora

A visualization project for the course DH2321 - Information visualization at KTH made together with Adam Cerven, Amanda Lindqvist, Solbritt Gateman and Wenjia Chen. AuraExplora is a tool that helps you plan your aurora borealis gazing using aurora forecasts, weather forecasts and daylight time.

PaintAR

A group project from the course DT2140 Multimodal Interaction and Interfaces, made together with Solbritt Gateman, Amanda Lindqvist and Johanna Lundqvist. We created an Android application where we used augmented reality together with image tracking to paint in AR using a smartphone.
