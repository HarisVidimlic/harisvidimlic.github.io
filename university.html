AuraExplora

A visualization project made in a Information Visualization course at KTH made together with Adam Cerven, Amanda Lindqvist, Solbritt Gateman and Wenjia Chen. AuraExplora is a tool that helps you plan your aurora borealis gazing using aurora forecasts, weather forecasts and daylight time. My focus was front-end development user testing.

PaintAR

A group project from a course about Multimodal Interaction and Interfaces, made together with Solbritt Gateman, Amanda Lindqvist and Johanna Lundqvist. We created an Android application where we used augmented reality together with image tracking to paint in AR using a smartphone. We used Unity together with Vuforia and the workload and task were evenly diveded amongs the group members.

First Person Tank Shooter with custom Level Of Detail system using distance and motion vectors

A group project together with Amanda Lindqvist where we implemened our own Level Of Detail system based on distance and velocity to a first person shooter game in Unity. We also used Blender to create additional meshes to use in our LOD system. This was made during a Computer Graphics and Interaction course.
